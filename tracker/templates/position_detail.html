Method & limitations

This projection resamples whole historical trading days at random from the last {{ sim.lookback_years }} years. Because the asset price and the exchange rate are drawn from the same day, their real co-movement is preserved rather than assumed away.

It assumes the future resembles the sampled past. It cannot produce a shock larger than any in that window, and a change in market regime would make these estimates unreliable. The figures are a scenario-based risk estimate, not a forecast.
